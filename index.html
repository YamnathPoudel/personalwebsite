About Me

As a dedicated science educator and educational innovator, I am committed to revolutionizing science education in Nepal through the integration of modern technology and innovative teaching methodologies. With over 15 years of experience, I have been at the forefront of educational transformation, leading initiatives that bridge the gap between traditional teaching methods and contemporary learning needs.

My approach combines hands-on experimentation, digital learning tools, and real-world applications to make science education more engaging and accessible to students across Nepal. As the President of the Science Teachers Association of Nepal - Lumbini Province, I work tirelessly to elevate the standard of science education and empower educators with modern teaching techniques.
